Our Mission: Digital Literacy for Everyone

Typeest is not just a typing tool. It is a free learning platform dedicated to students who want to master typing and computer skills without spending a single rupee.

Why we exist?

In the digital age, typing is a superpower. I created this platform to help students learn English and Hindi typing through a game-like experience. Our lessons are simple, structured, and 100% free forever.

What we offer?

We believe in sharing knowledge. Through this platform, I aim to teach not just typing, but core computer skills to help every student become tech-savvy.
